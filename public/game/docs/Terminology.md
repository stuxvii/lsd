Scripted Object: Scenes that can be instantiated which will affect gameplay with their actions.

LHitbox: Stands for Logical Hitbox. This is the hitbox that Nodes can trigger actions (Usually it's in the form of an [Area3D](https://docs.godotengine.org/en/stable/classes/class_area3d.html)).

PHitbox: Stands for Physical Hitbox. This is the hitbox that Nodes can physically collide with and interact with (Usually it's in the form of a [StaticBody3D](https://docs.godotengine.org/en/stable/classes/class_staticbody3d.html))

`global.gd`: Script file with logical variables. [Main article](https://lsdblox.cc/tuxgolf/documentation?page=global.gd.md)

PhysicsBody3D: It is a Physics Body in 3D space. Shocker, right? Included are: The Ball and The Player. Also referred to as PB3D.
