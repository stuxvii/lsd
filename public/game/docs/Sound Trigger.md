The Sound Trigger will make a sound play when a PhysicsBody3D or the Player

enters its trigger area.
<br>
<br>
One Time Use will make the trigger stop being triggerable after it's triggered (i hate typing these docs)
<br>
<br>
Replace Music will replace the level's BGM, and it requires you to assign the Player to it.
<br>
<br>
Persist Music will make the new music persist after soft-resets, 
<br>
the original BGM will be back after resetting without checkpoints or resetting the run
<br>
<br>
Ball Can Trigger will allow any PhysicsBody3D to trigger this object.
<br>
<br>
Time Position is measured in seconds as a floating point number.
