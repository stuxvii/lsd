The Spike Wall is a dangerous object that will harm the Player when it enters its LHitbox.
<br>
Like the Spike, it will deal 1 damage.
<br>
<br>
The Spike Wall can also rotate at a fixed speed, which may be determined at the moment of
<br>
placing it in its Speed Metadata property.
<br>
<br>
This object contains an LHitbox bigger than its PHitbox, assuring that the player will be
<br>
harmed when entering contact.
<br>
