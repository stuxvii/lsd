The Ball has a very simple purpose: To roll.

It is in the collision layers 1 and 2.
<br>
<br>
It has a mass of 500g, 0.5kg or 2.2lbs 

and is half a meter in radius.
<br>
<br>
Note that the ball has a slightly more different

PHitbox than what it may seem. (PHitbox is in blue)

![Image of the ball, its PHitbox is not perfectly aligned](/game/docs/image-11.png)

<br>
<br>
When the ball reaches a height of -40, it will be

considered "Inaccessible" and will trigger a Label

to be shown warning the User that they need to restart

to continue.
