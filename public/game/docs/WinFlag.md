The WinFlag is a teleporter to a new level. Whenever the Ball enters its LHitbox then it will

either send the Player to the next level, or to a level present in `global.map_list`.

Next Map: Boolean, will send the Player to the next level in order according to `global.current_map` and `global.current_world`.

World: Integer, determines what World the selected map will be in.

Map: Integer, will send the Player to a level inside of the Map inside of the selected World in `global.map_list`.
