### This page contains spoilers
The Boss is a scripted fight scene in which The Player will have to<br>
throw The Ball at The Boss 20 times in order to win.<br>
<br>
Stages:
<br>
1st stage, 20 health:
<br>
6 Machine Guns will come up which will target The Player
<br>
2nd stage, &lt;10 health:
<br>
The Boss will bring up a shield which will prevent The Ball from attacking.
<br>
Last stage, &lt;5 health:
<br>
The Boss will bring down the Super Mega Fuckin' Death Spike Wall.
<br>
<br>
The SMFDSW (Super Mega Fuckin' Death Spike Wall) is an amalgamation of Spike Walls,
<br>
all put together in a single instance in a human centipede fashion that can
<br>
kill The Player in seconds, and toss The Ball around.
